# Voice Protocol for this Repo

**For any future session that touches `src/content/*.md`, read this first — and then read the full skill at `cobalt-skills/writings-by-lala.md` in the Life-Dashboard repo before writing anything.**

## The one rule

Every post on this site must be **80% Lala's actual words** (translated from Taglish where needed) + **20% structural polish** (paragraph breaks, flow, sentence reordering).

**Zero invented sentences. Zero crafted imitation.**

## The structure

Every post follows the 7-step SWAHG storytelling formula from `life-dashboard-site/community.html` §8:

1. **Open with Vulnerability** — a specific raw sentence, not a thesis
2. **Paint the Scene** — sensory detail, where, when
3. **Name the Inner Critic** — in quoted voice, not summarized
4. **Pivot with Empathy** — "If you [specific experience], I have been there"
5. **Deliver Lesson via Story** — narrative, not bullets (except tactical Guide/Tool)
6. **Anchor to Faith** — scripture or principle that undergirds the lesson
7. **Close with Warm Invitation** — a low-friction action, never a hard sell

## The date rule

**Past only.** 2022-2024 typical for career reflections. 2016-2020 for origin stories. Never current year unless explicitly asked. Verify day-of-week with a calendar.

## The language rule

**English only.** Translate Taglish. Preserve sentence rhythm and Filipino English markers where they carry meaning.

## The close rule

End with a warm invitation, then `— Lala` on its own line. Never a hard CTA.
