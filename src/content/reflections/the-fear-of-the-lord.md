When I start my day at 4 AM, which is 3 PM Central Standard Time so I can work with my team, I always have fear.

Seven years I have been in this industry and the fear has not gone away.

But do you know what my secret is?

Every single morning, before I check my emails, before I think about the problems at work, I surrender everything to the Lord. "Lord, you take care of today." It is that simple.

And you know what happens? Peace comes to my heart out of nowhere.

Because this is what the Bible says. "The fear of the Lord is the beginning of wisdom."

Is that not powerful? When we acknowledge that we are not the source of all things, but God is, that is when true wisdom enters.

Let me ask you something. Are you afraid to start? Are you worried you will not be able to do it?

Here is the truth. The Lord never said we should not be afraid. What He is saying is, "Give me your fears, child."

You see, when we are creating proposals, when we are talking to potential clients, it is normal to be nervous. The difference is how we handle that fear.

I will not tell you it is easy. Six figures fast, easy like that. No. The journey is hard. Rejection hurts. Hearing that your work is not good enough hurts.

But here is my promise to you. When you process these challenges with the Lord, you will be surprised how far you have come.

Look at me. Seven years ago, if you had told me I would have an iMac, high-paying clients, and a platform to help others, I would not have believed you.

So remove the limiting beliefs. Take out the "I cannot do this" mindset.

Results take time. This is not magic.

It is a process, and God is with you every step of the way.

— Lala
