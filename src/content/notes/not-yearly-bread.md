Every single morning, before I check my emails, before I think about the problems at work, I surrender everything to the Lord.

"Lord, you take care of today." That is it. It is that simple.

And you know what happens? Peace comes to my heart out of nowhere.

Because this is what I keep coming back to in the Lord's prayer.

"Give us this day our daily bread."

Not yearly bread.

The Lord wants us day by day. He wants us to ask Him. He wants us to depend on Him.

Seven years I have been working in this industry and I still have fear every morning. I am still nervous. And I think that is how it is supposed to be. The Lord did not say we should not be afraid. What He is saying is, "Give me your fears, child."

So I give them. Every morning. Even when they keep coming back.

— Lala
