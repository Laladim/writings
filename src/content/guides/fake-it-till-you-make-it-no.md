I do not believe in fake it till you make it.

Let me tell you why.

God wants us to be excellent in everything we do. We cannot just bluff our way through. When you charge a client, you have to be deserving of that rate. You have to have prepared for it. You have to have the fear of God to deliver quality work.

A lot of courses right now are just about the money. That is not what we are doing here. We value excellence and integrity. A certification is not the goal. Real skills are the goal. Real growth. Real transformation. Because at the end of the day, the success is not just for us. It is for the glory of God.

Here is what I see happening when people skip the work.

Someone tells them to fake it till they make it. They charge a rate they cannot back up. The client's business suffers. Their own business suffers. And instead of actually committing to small steps, instead of doing the honest work, they are faking it, and the opportunity is wasted.

That is not what I want for you.

I am looking for people who are determined. I am not going to tolerate wrong practices, because I know what happens when we do.

Some people are selling courses because they want your money. They will hand you a certification that looks nice. But you do not need a certification. What you need is actual skill that you have developed. Skill you can show to a client. You understand the process. You understand the psychology. You understand the principle behind what you are doing.

That is real training.

If I am being honest, I would rather you take the longer road and be the real thing. Because results take time. It is not magic. It is a process. And God is with you every step of the way.

So before you charge, before you quote, before you put a number on your work, be deserving of it.

Then say the number with confidence.

— Lala
